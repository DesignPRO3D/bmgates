Who We Are

We specialise in the design, installation, repair and maintenance of automated gates, with over 30 years of combined experience.

At BM Gates and Automation Ltd all gate systems are designed and installed taking the following in to consideration: The security of your property, user convenience, all elements of safety and the need for the system to be aesthetically pleasing therefore enhancing the first impression of your home.

We install many different types of automated systems, ranging from timber hard wood 5 bar field gate, to ornate cast wrought iron gates. We also supply and install rising bollards and kerbs, barriers and garage doors.

Using our experience we are also able to offer special projects design and installation, this would include any unusual or one off bespoke designs and automation.
